Why VibroJaw?

Everything existing TMD solutions get wrong — solved in one wearable.

⟁

Detect

PVDF piezoelectric film on the temporalis muscle captures clenching vibration signatures, filtered from noise via a rolling average on the ESP32.

◎

Respond

On detection, vibration motors deliver a 50–100 Hz biofeedback pulse that interrupts the clenching reflex and relaxes the masseter musculature.

⊞

Track

Every event is timestamped and transmitted via WiFi to a live dashboard — the first longitudinal clenching health record for TMD patients.

⌖

Adjust

Detection thresholds and vibration parameters are configurable from the dashboard. Changes push to the ESP32 automatically within 10 seconds.
